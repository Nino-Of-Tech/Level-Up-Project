Business Info

LightEd is solving one of the largest yet most overlooked problems of the 21st century: electricity poverty. In an age when people are racing towards space, there are people who still struggle to keep their houses well-lit. Due to the scarcity of a sustainable and reliable alternative, they rely on products like candles, kerosene lights etc which is harmful to their health and to the environment and decreases their productivity. Lighted is consciously bringing a change to this harmful pattern by introducing a unique sustainable product that will provide enough of light and enable people in such communities live a quality life.
